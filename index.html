Seba Jun

A producer who redefined Hip Hop.

The Nujabes Sound

Nujabes's sound stands out in the hip hop world because he combined soulful jazz beats to create highly emotive pieces of music. The mellow beats of his music is both beautiful and relaxing. As such, you are likely to find some music pieces in lo-fi youtube playlists meant for studying and relaxing. If you would like to listen to his first album click on the video below.

A timeline of Nujabes's life

  • 1974 - Nujabes is born on Febreuary in Minato, Tokyo, Japan.
  • 1995 - Nujabes opens a record store.
  • 1998 - Nujabes begins to produce music.
  • 2003 - Nujabes releases his first solo album, Metaphorical Music, in August.
  • 2004 - The anime Samurai Champloo releases in May and features several tracks produced by Nujabes.
  • 2010 - Seba Jun passes away in February after a traffic accident in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan.
